I turn ambiguous, complex problems into products that are clear to use and hold up at scale.

I care about clarity, not just visual clarity but conceptual clarity: what's actually happening, and what someone should do next.

My craft sits between interaction design and system behavior. I'm drawn to ambiguous, 0-to-1 problems where the shape of the product isn't decided yet. I work closely with engineering and product, prototype early, design for the hard cases first, and keep the details holding up as things scale. Recently that has meant a lot of AI-driven surfaces, but the approach is the same whatever the technology.
