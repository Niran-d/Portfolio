I help teams turn complex, AI-driven systems into products people can actually trust.

I care a lot about clarity, not just visual clarity, but conceptual clarity. What's actually happening, what the model is doing, and what the person should do next.

My craft sits between interaction design and system behavior. I define what a model should return, how confidently it should return it, and what the interface should do next so people can decide without guessing. I work closely with engineering and data science, prototype behavior early, and design for recovery first: confidence cues, clean state transitions, and practical actions that map back to the product's core controls.
